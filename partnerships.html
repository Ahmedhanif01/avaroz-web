Our Partnerships

At Avaroz, we understand that partnerships are critical for the success of any business. We have built strong relationships with some of the leading companies in the industry to offer our clients the best possible solutions.

Mobile Operators

We have authorized channel partnerships with leading mobile operators in the Middle East, Pakistan, Bangladesh, and South Africa, such as Du, Etisalat, Zain, STC, Ooredoo, Jazz, Ufone, Zong, Telenor, and more. These partnerships allow us to offer direct carrier billing and mobile VAS services to our clients.

Payment Gateways

We have partnered with multiple payment gateways to provide seamless and secure payment integration for our clients. Our payment gateway partners include PayTabs, Telr, PayFort, and more.

Content Providers

We work with some of the world's leading content providers to bring the best VAS content to our clients. Our content providers include OnMobile, Nazara, Mobimedia, and more.

Product Partners

We have partnered with companies such as Codashop, Garena, MCP, IDEX, MAK United, MT2, and SLA to offer their products to our clients.

IT Service Providers

We have a strong network of IT service providers who help us provide web and application development, cloud services, and call center services to our clients.

Call Center Services

We provide cost-effective and reliable call center services to support our clients' business operations. Our call center services include inbound and outbound call handling, customer service, technical support, and order processing. Our experienced call center agents are trained to handle various customer inquiries and provide timely solutions.
We are always looking to expand our partnerships and offer our clients the latest and best solutions in the industry.
