Our Services

At Avaroz, we offer a range of services to cater to the diverse needs of our clients. Our services include

Mobile Value Added Services (VAS)

We are a leading aggregator of VAS content and services, providing a wide range of services such as SMS-based services, multimedia services, IVR services, and more. Our partnerships with top mobile operators in the Middle East, Pakistan, Bangladesh, and South Africa, enable us to provide seamless integration and monetization opportunities to our clients.

Direct Carrier Billing (DCB)

We offer DCB services that allow mobile users to purchase digital content and services through their mobile devices and have the cost charged directly to their mobile account. Our DCB platform supports multiple mobile operators and payment methods, making it a hassle-free and secure way to make purchases.

Payment Gateway Integration

Our payment gateway integration services enable our clients to accept payments through various payment methods, including mobile wallets, debit and credit cards, and bank transfers. We have connectivity with mobile operators, mobile wallets, and leading payment gateway providers in MENA and Pakistan, making it easier for our clients to reach their target audience.

IT Services

Our IT services include website development, social media management, search engine optimization, and online marketing. Our team of experienced professionals works closely with clients to understand their unique requirements and provide tailored solutions to meet their business needs.

Web and Application Development

We offer comprehensive web and application development services, including custom web design, development, testing, and deployment. Our expertise in various web technologies enables us to create scalable and secure applications that meet the latest industry standards.

Call Center Services

We provide cost-effective and reliable call center services to support our clients' business operations. Our call center services include inbound and outbound call handling, customer service, technical support, and order processing. Our experienced call center agents are trained to handle various customer inquiries and provide timely solutions.
